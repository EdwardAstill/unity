================================================================================
SUPPORTED UNITS (OVERVIEW)
================================================================================

Base Units:
   • Length: m, mm, cm, km, in, ft, yd, mi
   • Mass: kg, g, mg, t, lb, oz
   • Time: s, min, h, d, y

Derived Units:
   • Force: N (Newton) = kg m s-2
   • Pressure: Pa (Pascal) = kg m-1 s-2
   • Energy: J (Joule) = kg m2 s-2
   • Power: W (Watt) = kg m2 s-3
   • Frequency: Hz (Hertz) = s-1

(See reference.md for complete list)
